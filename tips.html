💡 Safe Browsing Tips

  • Always check links before clicking, especially from emails or social media.
  • Prefer HTTPS websites over HTTP for secure data transfer.
  • Avoid shortened or suspicious-looking URLs (e.g., bit.ly, tinyurl).
  • Do not download attachments from unknown sources.
  • Hover over links to preview the real destination before clicking.
  • Regularly update your browser and antivirus for better protection.
  • Use online tools like this Safe URL Checker to verify suspicious links.
